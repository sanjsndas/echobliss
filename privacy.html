Privacy Policy

Our Commitment to Your Privacy

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how EchoBliss Garden Design ("we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, discloses, and safeguards your information when you visit our website or use our services. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring that your personal information is handled responsibly. Please take time to read this policy carefully.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, such as when you request services, fill forms, subscribe to newsletters, or contact us. This includes your name, email, phone number, address, payment details, and any other information you choose to share. Additionally, we collect automated information through cookies and tracking technologies.

3. Use of Personal Information

The personal information we collect is used to provide, operate, and improve our services; communicate with you; process payments; send marketing materials when permitted; comply with legal obligations; and enhance your experience. We ensure that your data is used lawfully and only for the purposes described here.

4. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our website uses cookies and similar technologies to remember your preferences, analyze site usage, deliver personalized content, and improve functionality. You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings, but disabling cookies may affect some features or services.

5. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share information with trusted service providers who assist us in operating our business, complying with legal processes, or protecting our rights. These parties are obligated to keep your data confidential and use it only for the specified tasks.

6. Data Security

We implement comprehensive security measures using encryption, firewalls, secure servers, and regular monitoring to protect your information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is completely secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Data Retention

We retain your personal data only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy, comply with our legal obligations, resolve disputes, or enforce our agreements. Once the data is no longer needed, we securely delete or anonymize it to protect your privacy.

8. Your Rights and Choices

Depending on your location, you may have rights under applicable data protection laws, including access, correction, deletion, restriction, or data portability. You can manage your preferences, opt out of marketing emails, and withdraw consent where applicable. Contact us to exercise these rights.

9. Children’s Privacy

Our website and services are not intended for individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we learn that a child has provided us with personal data, we take steps to delete it promptly.

10. International Data Transfers

As a global company, your personal information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. We ensure appropriate safeguards, such as standard contractual clauses or compliance with recognized data privacy frameworks, to protect your data in transit and storage.

11. Third-Party Websites

Our website may contain links to third-party websites or services. We are not responsible for their privacy practices or content. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any third-party sites before providing them with your personal information.

12. Marketing and Communications

With your consent, we send promotional materials and updates about our services. You can opt out of these communications at any time by following the unsubscribe instructions provided in the emails or contacting us directly. We respect your preferences and will not send unwanted communications.

13. Analytics and Performance Monitoring

We use analytics tools to understand how visitors interact with our site, measure performance, and improve user experience. These tools may collect information such as IP addresses, device information, and browsing behavior in an aggregated and anonymized form.

14. Data Accuracy and Updates

It is important that the personal information we hold is accurate and up to date. Please notify us promptly if your information changes so we can maintain reliable records and provide you with effective services.

15. Legal Basis for Processing

We rely on various legal bases for processing your personal data, including your consent, contractual necessity, legitimate interests, and compliance with legal obligations. Where required, we will obtain your explicit consent before processing sensitive data.

16. Privacy Policy Updates

We may update this policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices, legal requirements, or technological advances. We encourage you to review this page regularly for the latest information. Continued use of our site after any changes indicates acceptance of the updated policy.

17. Contact Information

If you have questions, concerns, or requests regarding this privacy policy or your personal information, please contact us at info@echoblissgarden.com or by phone at +1 (833) 667-8898. We are committed to addressing your privacy needs promptly and transparently.

18. Use of Cookies

Cookies are small data files placed on your device to help us provide a better user experience, remember your preferences, enable core functionality, and analyze site traffic. We do not use cookies to collect sensitive personal information without your explicit consent.

19. Managing Cookies

Most browsers allow you to control or delete cookies through their settings. You can refuse cookies, but this may affect website functionality or reduce your browsing experience. We provide cookie notices and opt-in mechanisms where required by law.

20. Data Protection Officer

We have appointed a Data Protection Officer responsible for overseeing our compliance with data protection laws and this privacy policy. You may contact the officer via the contact information provided to address privacy-related questions or complaints.

21. Children's Online Privacy Protection

We comply with the Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and similar regulations. We do not knowingly collect, maintain, or use personal information from children under 13 years of age. If you believe we have inadvertently collected data from a child, please contact us immediately to request removal.

22. User-generated Content

If you submit content to our website, such as reviews or comments, we collect any personal information included with your submission. Content you provide becomes publicly available and is governed by this privacy policy and our terms of use.

23. Third-Party Service Providers

We engage trusted third-party service providers to facilitate our business operations including hosting, analytics, and payment processing. These providers are contractually obligated to maintain the confidentiality and security of your information.

24. Security Breach Notification

In the unlikely event of a data breach affecting your personal information, we will promptly notify you and regulators as required by law, detailing the nature of the breach and steps taken to mitigate harm.

25. Cross-border Transfers

Where your personal information is transferred across borders, we ensure that adequate safeguards are in place, such as standard contractual clauses or compliance with recognized international privacy frameworks, to protect your data rights.

26. Retention of Personal Data

We retain your personal information only for as long as necessary for the purposes described, including legal or regulatory obligations, dispute resolution, and enforcement of agreements. We use secure methods to delete or anonymize data that is no longer required.

27. Third-Party Advertising

We do not use third-party advertising on our website. However, affiliated sites or partners may collect data to deliver targeted ads. We encourage you to review their privacy policies if you engage on such third-party platforms.

28. Social Media Features

Our website may include social media features such as sharing buttons or feeds. These may collect information and track interactions. We do not control their privacy practices and recommend reviewing the privacy policies of those social platforms.

29. Marketing Consents

Where required by law, we seek your explicit consent before sending marketing messages. You can withdraw your consent at any time by following the opt-out instructions in communications or by contacting us directly.

30. Email Communications

We use your email to send order confirmations, service updates, newsletters, and promotional offers with your permission. Emails include unsubscribe options compliant with relevant laws.

31. Use of Aggregated Data

We may use aggregated, non-identifiable data to analyze trends, improve our services, and better understand our customers' needs. This data cannot be used to identify individual users.

32. Data Accuracy and Access

You are entitled to access the personal data we hold about you and to request corrections to ensure its accuracy. Please contact us with any data access or correction requests.

33. Data Portability

Where applicable, you may request your personal data in a structured, commonly used format to transfer it to another service provider or for your records.

34. Restriction and Objection

You have rights to restrict or object to certain data processing activities, including direct marketing or profiling. These rights are subject to specific legal conditions.

35. Automated Decision-Making

We do not use automated decision-making or profiling that produces legal effects or significantly affects you.

36. Links to Other Websites

Our website may contain links to other websites for your convenience. We are not responsible for their privacy practices. You should review each site’s privacy policy separately.

37. Your Responsibilities

We encourage you to protect your personal data by using strong passwords, avoiding sharing login details, and promptly informing us of any unauthorized use.

38. Complaints

If you have concerns about our privacy practices, you may submit a complaint to us or the relevant data protection authorities. We are dedicated to resolving complaints efficiently.

39. Governing Law

This Privacy Policy and related data processing are governed by the laws of the jurisdiction in which EchoBliss Garden Design operates. Any disputes will be resolved in accordance with applicable laws.

40. Effective Date

This Privacy Policy is effective as of the date stated at the top of the page and remains in effect until amended. Your continued use of our services signifies your acceptance of any updated policies.
